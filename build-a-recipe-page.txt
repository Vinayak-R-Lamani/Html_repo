    <h1>Chocolate Chip Cookies</h1>
    <p>These delicious chocolate chip cookies are perfect for a quick snack or a special treat. Crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, they’re sure to be a hit!</p>
    
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1 cup unsalted butter, softened</li>
      <li>1 cup white sugar</li>
      <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>2 cups chocolate chips</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
      <li>In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.</li>
      <li>Gradually mix in the flour until fully incorporated.</li>
      <li>Fold in the chocolate chips, scoop dough onto baking sheets, and bake for 10-12 minutes.</li>
    </ol>
